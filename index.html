About

Hello, I'm Dr. Mo El-Haj. I'm the Director of VinNLP Research Group and a Reader (Associate Professor) in NLP at the College of Engineering & Computer Science, VinUniversity, Hanoi, Vietnam. Currently, I'm on a visiting research role at Lancaster University, but I'm based in Hanoi, Vietnam. I am the Director of the VinUniversity NLP Group. My doctoral research, completed at The University of Essex, focused on Arabic Multi-document Summarisation. I specialise in Summarisation, Information Extraction, Financial NLP, and multilingual NLP, with a particular focus on languages such as English, Arabic, Spanish, Portuguese, Welsh, among others. I'm particularly passionate about under-resourced languages and the development of NLP datasets.

Research Interests

Natural Language Processing (NLP), Language Resources, Data Science, Health and Medicine, Biomedical NLP, Text Summarization, Corpus and Computational Linguistics, Financial NLP and FinTech, Machine Learning on Text Classification, Crowd-sourcing and Information Extraction.
