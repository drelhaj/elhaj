USAS Lexicon Translation Form

Do you speak English, Italian, Portuguese or Chinese? We need your help!
As part of the SenseSourcing UCREL project, we are running "trial for now" experiments asking speakers from the languages above to label words with tags (labels) sensing their meaning with no more than 10 tags per word as part of an effort to translate the UCREL USAS semantic tagger's lexicon into new languages.

Please click on any of the sample links from your language(s) below.

****NOTE****

Please write back to us at usas.ucrel@gmail.com with a feedback on the following: (clicking the email link will have the info below auto-filled in the email body, please delete as appropriate)

1-How easy do you find following the instructions? (v. easy, easy, okay, difficult, v. difficult)

2-Do you find the user interface easy to use? (yes, no)

3-How long did it take you to read the instructions, complete and submit the task? (in minutes)

4-Comments on encoding problems where the text (non-English) does not read correctly.
