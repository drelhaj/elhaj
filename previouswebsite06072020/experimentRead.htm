Assessing Text Readability

We are looking for Arabic and English native speakers (10 each) to help with assessing the readability of Arabic and English documents.
The documents are less than a page long each. Participants will have to read through and rank the documents according to how readable they are on a 5 point Likert scale - Very Low, Low, Medium, High, Very High.

The scores reflect the ease of read with "Very High" meaning very easy to read and "Very Low" very difficult to read.

If interested please download the documents below:
- You can do any language as long as you are a native speaker
- If you are an Arabic native speaker please consider doing Arabic_1 and Arabic_2 (they are the same set of documents instead that Arabic_2 documents are written with diacritics (Harakat/Tashkeel)
- Please fill the score sheet on page 1 and email the answers specifying which language you worked on to Mahmoud El-Haj m.el-haj@Lancaster.ac.uk

English Documents (10 documents, 1 on each page)
Arabic without Diacritics [Arabic_1] (10 documents, 1 on each page)
Arabic with Diacritics [Arabic 2] (10 documents, 1 on each page)
