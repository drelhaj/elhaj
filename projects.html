AI, NLP & Financial AI Research Projects

My research projects span Financial AI, multilingual NLP, trustworthy AI, low-resource language technologies, document intelligence, generative AI, and large language models. These projects involve collaborations with universities, governments, regulators, and industry partners across the UK, Europe, the Middle East, and Southeast Asia. Many of the projects focus on building scalable AI infrastructure, multilingual datasets, explainable AI systems, and domain-specific language technologies for high-stakes environments including finance, policy, healthcare, and sustainability. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}
